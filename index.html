About Me

I started my career as a photographer. I eventually made my way into ecommerce product photography. Working for a small .com, I needed to learn a little html and css to get the products online.

One thing led to another, and I quickly realised I was much happier analyzing the performance and interactions of people with our site.

I have worked on ecommerce sites as an analyst since 2012. I decided to become a web developer because I needed to understand what was going on under the hood. This allowed me the skills I need to both tag and track what matters most me. In modern ecommerce, optimization is key. Now I am a full stack ecommerce developer who understands the "why" as well as the "how".
